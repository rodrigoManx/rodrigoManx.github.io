Conclusions

The longer the duration of the game, the more likely the Dire team wins, this is demonstrated by the increase in Dire's win rate when the games are longer. This is probably due to the characteristics of the pitch that offer more space to the Radiant team, so they are more aggressive.

There is a hero that is usually present in all the long games, his name is Techies and his abilities seem to be defensive.

Thanks to the possibility of filtering the games by heroes, you can search for teams with a good index of victories. For example the heroes Omnikight, Dazzle and Shadow Fiend together have a victory index of 67.74%. Another possibility with the heroes filter is to look for counter picks.

Having two or three support heroes elevates a team's chances of winning. For example of the three heroes mentioned above, the first two are supports and as we see they have a high victory rate.
